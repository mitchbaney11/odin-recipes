The Ancient Way

"Although none of record have lived through this rite, it is said to have delicious notes of walnut, coffee and brown sugar

Making this recipe takes quite some time and some hard to find ingredients.

Ingredients

  • The Soul of a Ginger(Candied Ginger works too)
  • Black Root Stew(Or your favorite bourbon)
  • 2 pinches of star dust(Or .5 oz of simple syrup)
  • Slogroth Venom(Or black walnut bitters, two dashes)
